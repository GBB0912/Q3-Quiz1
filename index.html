Batman Begins


Batman Begins is the first movie in the trilogy of Christopher Nolan. It introduces Bruce Wayne and his alter ego, Batman. When he was a child, he did martial arts training and was taught by Henri Ducard, which will be his enemy later. After that, he returned to Gotham City, and asked the help of his butler, ALfred, and to help him in creating the weapons that will him in beating the most-wanted criminals of Gotham when he hears about someone putting substances on people and make them have fears in their head. He went on to fight that 'someone' and tried everything in his power to arrest that 'someone', which happen to be Scarecrow.


4.5/5
